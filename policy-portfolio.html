Featured Report

Data Localization in Pakistan

Developed for the Asia Internet Coalition and LIRNEasia Solutions, this work examines the legal, regulatory, economic, and sectoral implications of data localization requirements in Pakistan. It assesses how localization could affect trade, business costs, cybersecurity risks, and Pakistan's digital economy.

Report

Regulatory Risks for Internet Companies: Pakistan, Vietnam, Indonesia, Singapore

This work assesses regulatory risks for internet companies across four Asian jurisdictions, focusing on content moderation, data privacy, cybersecurity, and platform governance. It includes legal analysis, public-domain research, and stakeholder interviews to evaluate regulatory risks and proposes mitigation strategies for content-hosting platforms.

Report

Effective Targeting Mechanisms for Emergency Cash Transfers in Pakistan

Developed for the International Growth Centre Pakistan, this analysis examines how emergency cash transfer programs can better identify vulnerable households during crises. We evaluate targeting mechanisms, administrative constraints, and data systems to support a more shock-responsive social protection system.
