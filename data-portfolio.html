Featured Project

Effect of Congestion Pricing Policy on For-Hire Vehicles' Mobility in New York City

This work evaluates the effects of New York City's congestion pricing policy on for-hire vehicle mobility patterns using over 573 million trip records from the NYC Taxi and Limousine Commission. It employs a difference-in-differences approach to estimate how congestion pricing changes trip demand, travel behavior, and spatial mobility.

Music Recommendation System Based on Spotify Audio Features

A content-based music recommendation system that recommends songs using Spotify audio features such as danceability, energy, valence, acousticness, and tempo. Includes an interactive app and explores feature engineering and similarity-based recommendations.

Adaptive Testing for Data Science Recruitment

An adaptive testing platform for junior data scientist recruitment that uses Item Response Theory to dynamically select questions based on a candidate's estimated ability level. The project was deployed as an interactive Shiny app.
